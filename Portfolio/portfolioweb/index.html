About

Hello my name is Alexandros Tsistrakis, I am from Greece and currently I am living and studying in Edinburgh. Creativity is something that I will never stop being passionate about. My interest in art started at very young age, where I was enjoying creative activities like drawing, coloring, cutting and crafting.

After finishing high school I studied Video Game Programming at the Institute of Vocational Training of Agios Dimitrios in Athens. This was my first introduction to more advanced digital media editing. Along with my studies, online videos, live streams and books are my main learning sources. I have seen my skills improving dramatically every month, that motivates me to progress further.

Skills

At the moment, I am on the final academic year of HND Interactive Media Design course at Edinburgh College. This course has guided me to acquire skills on 3D modeling, 2D animation, web design, photography, and video editing. The knowledge on various interactive media gave me a better understanding about art, and enhanced my imagination. The idea of creating something unique, something conceived, planned and developed is what truly inspires me.
